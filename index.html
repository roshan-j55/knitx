Our Product

AI Fabric Defect Detection System

A plug-and-play edge AI camera module that mounts directly onto your existing production lines.

  • Real-time Detection (10-15 FPS)

    High-speed inference on edge devices ensures defects are caught immediately without slowing down production.

  • Hole & Needle Line Detection

    Specifically trained Convolutional Neural Networks to identify the most common knitting defects.

  • Defect Sizing & Positioning

    Accurately logs the physical dimensions and exact coordinates of each defect on the roll.

LED + Buzzer Alerts

Immediate on-floor notifications for severe defects to halt production lines instantly.

QR-Based Reporting

Generates unique QR codes for each roll containing the full defect log for downstream QA.

Cloud & Local Storage

Flexible data retention. Store logs locally on edge devices or sync to the cloud for global analytics.
